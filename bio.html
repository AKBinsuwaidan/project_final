About Me

My name is Abdulrahman, and I am a 25-year-old university student who also works in our family business. Balancing academics with professional responsibilities, I find joy in my tight schedule, always seeking to grow and learn in every aspect of my life. Football is not just a sport to me; it's a passion that captivates my heart, much like my fascination with new technologies that promise to shape our future. In my free time, I dive into the worlds created by movies and books, exploring narratives that stretch my imagination and deepen my understanding of the world around me. Yet, beyond the boundaries of stories and screens, I harbor a profound desire to travel the world. It's a goal that pushes me to see, experience, and understand the vastness and variety of the globe. So far, I've reached 12% of this goal, a testament to my dedication and the journey ahead.
